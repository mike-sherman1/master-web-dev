Major Lazer Drops New EP

Electronic music trio Major Lazer just dropped a new EP titled "Know No Better." It features six fresh tracks with Caribbean / Latin vocals. The leading single of the same title features Camila Cabello, Travis Scott, and Quavo.

Known for their chart-topping single "Lean On" released in May 2015, Major Lazer is a staple in the mainstream house world. If the summer of 2015 is anything to go by, you won't even be able to turn on the pop radio stations without hearing their new single!

New Seven Lions EP Coming Soon

Seven Lions is releasing an EP with 8 tracks on June 26th. Called "Where I Won't Be Found," a single of the same name was released June 5th for our listening pleasure. Featuring vocals from NÉONHÈART, the song is a lot more mellow and feel-good than some of Seven Lion's dubstep mixes.

Known for his heavy and melodic tracks, Seven Lions is a pillar of the EDM community. He has performed at SXSW, Ultra Music Festival, and many others. He was nominated for DJ Times' America's Best DJ award in 2014.
